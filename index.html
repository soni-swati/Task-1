    School Environment Description

    A school is a place where students come to gain knowledge, grow in discipline, and build their future. The environment of a good school is clean, safe, friendly, and full of learning opportunities.

    Our School has rules to maintain order and discipline. Some common rules are:

    • Wear the school uniform properly.
    • Be respectful to teachers and classmates.
    • No mobile phones in class.
    • Arrive on time.
    • Complete homework regularly.
    • Keep the school clean and avoid littering.
    • No fighting or bullying.
    Following these rules helps create a peaceful and productive learning space for everyone.

    Students

    Students are the heart of the school. They come from different backgrounds and attend school to study, make friends, and develop their skills. They wear uniforms, follow a timetable, and participate in different activities like sports, music, and arts. Good students are respectful, punctual, and eager to learn.

    Teachers

    Teachers are the pillars of the school. They guide students, explain lessons, and help them understand the world. A good teacher is kind, patient, and always ready to help. Teachers also prepare exams, check homework, and encourage students to do their best.

    Principal

    The principal is the head of the school. They are responsible for managing all school activities, staff, and student discipline. A principal ensures that the school runs smoothly and that everyone follows the rules. They often meet with teachers, students, and parents to solve problems and improve the school environment.
